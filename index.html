What I Do

Define, Design and Decide | Deploy

These are what I call the 3D's

Define

First we define our problem in a data science approachable way. Determine the type and amount of data available and how it can be used to unfold our problem.

Design

Here I explore, clean and transform our data. Then Visualize the data to find correlations and trends. And then we get predictive by modeling our data to see if we can automate this process or gain insight on the future.

Decide | Deploy

If our model was designed to help us gain insight here we use our visualizations and predictions to decide on a course of action based on our defined problem.

If our model was designed to automate a process or complete a task we deploy our model and start again from step 1 to improve it.
